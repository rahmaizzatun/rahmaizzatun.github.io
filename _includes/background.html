Background

I’m a fresh Accounting graduate from Jenderal Soedirman University with a strong interest in how numbers reflect reality and how audits ensure accountability. During my internship at a public accounting firm, I assisted in external audits across various industries—supporting stock opname, reviewing financial statements, and tracing evidence to its source.

From these experiences, I learned that auditing is both a responsibility and an art. Each audit tells a story not just of numbers, but of integrity, risk, and trust. I value the precision and critical thinking required in the process.

Outside academics, I volunteer as a BIPA Assistant at the International Relations Office, helping international students adapt and learn Bahasa Indonesia. I enjoy cultural exchange and believe curiosity and open-mindedness are key to both personal and professional growth.
